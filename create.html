    The Product Number identifies the specific item or material associated with the Non-Conformance Report (NCR), ensuring precise tracking and resolution of the issue.

    The Sales Order Number identifies the specific customer order associated with this Non-Conformance Report (NCR). It helps link the issue to the original order for accurate tracking and resolution.

    Quantity received indicates the total quantity of items received by the Quality department for inspection as part of the NCR process.

    Quantity Defective Represents the quantity of items found to be defective or non-conforming during the Quality inspection.

    The Item description provides a detailed description of the product or material associated with the Non-Conformance Report (NCR) to clearly identify the item in question.

    Description of defect provides a detailed explanation of the issue or problem found with the product or material during the inspection. This helps clearly define the nature of the defect for corrective actions and follow-up.

    Allows you to attach images or videos related to the defect or non-conformance. This can include photos of the defective items or videos demonstrating the issue, providing visual evidence to support the NCR.
